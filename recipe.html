Simple Recipe Ideas

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp sunflower oil
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • Thumb-sized piece of ginger, grated
  • 6 chicken thighs, boneless and skinned
  • 3 tbsp medium spice paste (tikka works well)
  • 400g can chopped tomatoes
  • 100g Greek yogurt
  • 1 small bunch of coriander, leaves chopped
  • 50g ground almonds
  • Naan bread or cooked basmati rice, to serve

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a flameproof casserole dish or large frying pan over medium heat. Add the onion and a generous pinch of salt and fry for 8-10 mins, or until the onion has softened and started to turn golden.
  2. Chop the chicken into chunky 3cm pieces, add to the pan and fry for 5 mins before stirring through the spice paste and tomatoes, along with 250ml water.
  3. Bring to the boil, lower to a simmer, and cook on gentle heat uncovered for 25-30 mins or until the chicken is tender and the sauce has thickened.
  4. Remove the pan from the heat and stir in the Greek yogurt, chopped coriander, and ground almonds.
  5. Serve with naan bread or basmati rice.
